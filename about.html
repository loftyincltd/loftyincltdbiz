About LoftyInc

Innovation, Execution & Craft.

LoftyInc is an Innovation development company with vast experience in Business and Project development. At loftyInc, we facilitate impact projects in sub-saharan Africa.

With keen interest in MSMEs, social impact is at the core of our existence at LoftyInc; we proffer innovative solutions to social impact problems. We are motivated to leverage technology to solve social impact problems across Sub-Saharan Africa.

Our Services
At LoftyInc our core values are Collaboration, Excellence & Social Impact
Our work goes beyond single projects or initiatives

We strive to deliver impeccable services for our clients.

We always develop, consult and execute projects using best-in class project management methodologies.

We provide the training, mentorship, and support needed to sustain a culture of innovation. By opening up the innovation process, we bring together diverse organizations and stakeholders—including the individuals and communities that will benefit from the work.

What motivates us at LoftyInc is to facilitate and create technology solutions that can be leveraged to drive economic development and social impact in West Africa starting with Nigeria.
02 What we seek to achieve (Mission)
